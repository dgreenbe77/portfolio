About

Hello,
I’m Malvina, a French designer now living and studying in San Francisco. I am interested in working in web design, user experience, publications and type-based work. I also enjoy illustrating as a hobby.

I have a background in Applied Arts and a degree in Fashion Design, which makes me a versatile person with interest and knowledge in many design disciplines. I enjoy all things organized and I like organizing everything. I am considered efficient and fast-paced, but I always make sure things are done right.

For the past year I have been employed as a mentor. At the same time, I have been working at the school's design studio, Emerge. These experiences have helped me become a better designer and communicator, by teaching me how to collaborate, give and receive feedback, and understand the needs of the clients.
